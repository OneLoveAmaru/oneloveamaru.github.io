Office hours for consultation are arranged by appointment. Nancy is a provider with Medicare, Aetna, and Health Now. Other insurance companies may or may not cover the services of a Registered Dietitian (RD) even with a physician referral. If your insurance company does not cover Medical Nutrition Therapy as provided by a RD, Nancy will be glad to review various alternative options for you.
